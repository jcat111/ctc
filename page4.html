The flaming pepper thrower

the flaming pepper thrower (tfpt) is like a flame thrower but the flaming pepper thrower shoots out flaming pepper spray. you may be wondering "how does the pepper spray catch on fire?" well it's simple we made flammable pepper spray.





homing molotov launcher

the homing molotov launcher is the new and improved version of the first one (the molotov launcher). You might wonder "what does it do that is different from the first?" Well it's simple we added a homing feature to it and you don't have to build the molotov.





sleepy shield


The sleepy shield makes anyone go to sleep with even the slightest of touches. you may be wondering "won't the wielder fall asleep?" they won't because the affects only happen when they get hit with the front of the sleepy shield.
